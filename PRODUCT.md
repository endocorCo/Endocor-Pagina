# ENDOCOR — Product Context

register: brand

## Product Purpose

Premium digital studio website. ENDOCOR designs and builds high-end websites, landing pages, and digital presence for luxury and aspirational brands in the Mexican market. The site itself is the portfolio and pitch — every interaction is a demonstration of craft.

## Users

- Mexican business owners and brand managers evaluating web studios
- Luxury brand founders (fashion, hospitality, health) seeking a studio that matches their aesthetic level
- Referral visitors who have seen ENDOCOR's work on a client site
- Age: 28–50. Expectation: premium, not corporate. They will notice bad typography.

## Brand Voice

Calm, precise, confident. Never loud. Never playful. The aesthetic says "we know what we're doing and we don't need to prove it." Technical + refined + restrained. Think: a high-end architecture firm's business card, not a startup pitch deck.

Three concrete brand-voice words: **mechanical, distilled, assured**.

## Aesthetic Commitments

- Fonts: Michroma (display, technical geometric) + Outfit (body, clean humanist). These are committed brand fonts — do not replace.
- Color: Navy (#1B232B) primary, sage green (#8DA67A) accent, mist/steel as text hierarchy, sand as warmth. Restrained strategy.
- Motion: Subtle, purposeful. Staggered reveals, parallax, micro-interactions. Never flashy.
- Density: Editorial. Intentionally small type with strong tracking on labels. Luxury density, not consumer spaciousness.

## Anti-References

- NOT SaaS landing page (hero metric template, feature grids, social proof ticker spam)
- NOT startup brutalism (raw type, visible grids, heavy borders as decoration)
- NOT Fraunces/serif editorial (wrong lane — ENDOCOR is technical, not literary)
- NOT purple gradients, glass cards everywhere, or neon
- NOT warm/friendly design studio (they're precise, not approachable)

## Strategic Principles

- Design IS the proof of capability. Every section must demonstrate taste.
- Restraint is the flex. Less is only more when it's intentional.
- The portfolio section sells. Everything else supports it.
- Spanish-language content, Mexican cultural context.
